The wikilink bug

Alongside the orphans, one unresolved wikilink lurked in Claude Obsidian Integration. The page contained a literal bracket-bracket token inside backticks, meant as documentation of the syntax. Obsidian parses wikilinks inside backticks anyway, so it created a phantom link to a non-existent page.

Fix attempt 1 changed the brackets to a hyphenated form — same problem, different name. Fix attempt 2 rephrased the line entirely: "compiles concepts into wiki/ pages with Obsidian-style cross-references." No brackets, no phantom link.

Never write bracket-bracket-anything in a wiki page when you mean to refer to the syntax — even inside backticks or code fences, Obsidian still resolves it.

The backlink-loop rule

Every single orphan was created during a compile pass. The pages linked outward (so they have context) but nothing linked inward (so they're invisible to the graph). The fix is a process change: step 3b.

Before — orphan factory

The compile workflow without step 3b.

Step 1
Read raw clippings, extract concepts
Step 2
Create/update wiki page with outgoing links
Step 3
Declare compile done
Step 3b — missing
No back-reference step. New page is a dead end.
Result
10 orphans accumulate per run

After — backlink loop

The compile workflow with step 3b.

Step 1
Read raw clippings, extract concepts
Step 2
Create/update wiki page with outgoing links
Step 3a — new
Add one back-reference from the most relevant parent page
Step 3b — new
Verify: does the new page have at least 1 incoming link?
Result
Zero orphans. Every page reachable from day one.
